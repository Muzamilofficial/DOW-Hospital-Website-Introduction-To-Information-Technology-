Center of Executive Check-Up
Considering the growing health problems in the populated mega city of Karachi, the Dow University of Health Sciences has now planned to establish a center where people of every stratum, elite to middle class, can come for regular checkups for the detection of diseases and problems, at the early curable stage e.g. early detection of cancer in females and males. In our population, there is very little practice of regular checkups for the screening of cancer like diseases; hence, patients usually show up in late untreatable stages of the disease.

Location:
This clinic is located in the heart of the city, adjacent to Liaquat National Hospital, near Aga Khan Hospital on Stadium road for the convenience of the general public.

Instructions for Patients
Prior appointment must be taken at least one day before the desired check up by registering name and date of birth at the executive clinic.

At least twelve hours fasting is required for the blood test, only water can be taken during the fasting period.

Sterilized bottles must be collected from the Dow Lab collection center for urine tests one day before the appointment at executive clinic.

Consultation by the specialist will be provided within a week.

Reports of Investigations will also be sent on the given email within 2 weeks of consultation.

Company sponsored patients must bring authorized letter along with then consultation.
