Prospective Students


“ENTER TO LEARN, LEAVE TO SERVE”

If you are considering joining Dow University of Health Sciences, this is the right time to apply and challenge yourself to pursue a career in one of the top tiered medical universities recognized worldwide. The university offers undergraduate, post graduate and doctoral programs including Bachelors, Masters, MPhil and PhD programs, along with Diploma and Short Courses in all disciplines of the Medical and allied Sciences, for national as well as international students.

We are home to renowned doctors, consultants and specialists working within the country and abroad. We aim to produce proficient workers that emerge as bright graduates, the best skilled doctors and well-rounded professionals to contribute to the delivery of quality patient care.

Campus Life

There are two campuses of Dow University; one is situated at the heart of the city main in Sadder, Karachi, which is the main Campus, while the other is known as the OJHA campus, which is situated near Suparco, Gulzar-e-Hijri, Karachi. The campus has state of the art comfortable, yet stimulating living and learning environment, with virtually every amenity. Dow University provides its students a friendly atmosphere at the campus, indulging them in different educational and recreational activities, while providing numerous facilities for their comfort and facilities for their academics.


Transport Facilities

University transport facility is available on nominal charges for all students, faculty and staff of the Dow University. These DUHS points run almost on all routes of the city. The transportation system consists of large fleet of buses that emphasizes upon an efficient, reliable service maintaining punctuality with a safe, sustainable and convenient ride providing ease and comfort to the passengers while commuting.
