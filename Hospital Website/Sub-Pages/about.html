Dr. Kashif Shafique MBBS (Dow), MPH (Glasgow), PhD (Glasgow), Head, Department of Research Dow University of Health Sciences
Message for
Department of Research, DUHS, Ojha Campus,
Karachi

Message By Director Research,Dr. Kashif Shafique,Dow University of Health Sciences, Karachi.

It is a matter of great honor for me, as head of the research, to introduce you the activities of Department of Research at Dow University of Health Sciences. Over the past few years, Dow University of Health Sciences has expanded exponentially with the establishment of its various associated institutions.

Research is indispensable for the field of health sciences because the medical facilities available today are the result of research efforts of several decades of health sciences, practitioners and professionals. The assiduous efforts of health sciences researches have made it possible to reveal the real causes that underpin fatal diseases and research has led to the discovery of miraculous medicines and approaches to treatment of diseases.

I believe that faculty of DUHS has dual responsibility of teaching and training of students as well as contributing to the development of health sciences and healthcare in Pakistan.
Dedicated faculty and staff at the Department of Research will continue its support for researchers and guide the upcoming students for producing high quality and impactful research.
OBJECTIVES OF DUHS - DICE HEALTH
1): To foster culture of Innovation and Entrepreneurship in the country and make it part of Nation’s DNA.

2): To establish a strong linkage between academia and health Industry and provide a common platform for academia, industry, government, entrepreneurs and expatriates to interact, share knowledge and collaborate on innovations and commercialization, in order to grow the indigenous health industry in the country.

3): To create a positive and favorable image of Pakistan health industry in the eyes of international community – i.e. country fully capable of developing innovative health products and provide health services to the rest of the world.

4): To expedite the process of technology revolution in Pakistan by motivating both academia and industry to acquire, promote and utilize state-of-art technologies / high-tech software for the rapid development of innovative health products & services.

5): Clinical Trials Unit (CTU), Dow University of Health Sciences (DUHS), is conducting clinical trials (sponsor and investigator initiated) with a broad expertise in planning, execution and interpretation of research projects. CTU provides focused comprehensive planning leading to successful trials.
