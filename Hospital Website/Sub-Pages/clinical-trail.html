Clinical Trails Unit

Clinical Trials Unit (CTU) was established in March 2009 to support all aspects of clinical trials, from concept to analysis & reporting. In August 2009, the Ministry of Health (MoH), Pakistan has identified DUHS as one of the site of clinical trials centers in the province of Sindh.

CTU provides GCP compliant trial infrastructure for Principal Investigator (PI) and the link between PI and industry, which is then supported by training programs for clinical trials management.

Clinical Trials Unit (CTU), Dow University of Health Sciences (DUHS), is conducting clinical trials (sponsor and investigator initiated) with a broad expertise in planning, execution and interpretation of research projects. CTU provides focused comprehensive planning leading to successful trials. CTU-DUHS has a complete resource solution on a single platform, with facilities from initial working till the statistical data entry, analysis and interpretation.


Vision
To establish CTU, as a center for clinical trials in Pakistan, ensuring quality and integrity in compliance with International Conference on Harmonization (ICH)- Good Clinical Practice (GCP) Guidelines


Mission
To perform clinical trials efficiently and professionally in clinical research following the International Directives for clinical trial research (Declaration of Helsinki, ICH, and GCP Guidelines). To strengthen cooperation and collaboration between industry, academia and government. To establish training programs to develop the next generation of clinical investigators and clinical trials personnel.

Introductory Symposium Held on 09.12.2017

Introductory Symposium to 16th Clinical Research Certified Professional Course was held on Saturday, 09th December, 2017 at Atta Ur Rehman Lecture Hall on the occasion of Certificate Distribution Ceremony to the successful students of 15th CRCP Course.. The Program was started with the recitation of few verses from Holy Quran.

Prof. Bader Faiyaz Zuberi, Director Clinical Trials Unit (CTU) gave Welcome address and highlighted the importance of Clinical Research & explained the facilities for Clinical Trials at DUHS. Dr. Fakhsheena Anjum Associate Professor Dow College of Pharmacy presented the outline of CRCP. A Questions/Answers session was also arranged to provide individuals information and help in seeking position as Clinical Research Associate.

Prof. Bader Faiyaz Zuberi and Dr. Fakhsheena Anjum Distributed Certificate among the successful students.
